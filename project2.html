Automated Deployable Robust Training (ADROIT) System, Phase II

This project is conducted in collaboration with engineers from the Barron Assoc. company to 1) develop an automated training system to train rodents to detect explosives or other harmful compounds of interest, 2) develop a system to monitor and transmit real-time data (i.e., physiologic variables, vocalizations, motion patterns, body orientation, and location) from the animals to a computing base station, and 3) develop a methodology to direct the animals during a search in the exterior environment such as a mine field with minimal human involvement.

(Funded by research support from Army STTR PH II Project, Barron Associates & Williams, C. L., Univ. of Virginia).
