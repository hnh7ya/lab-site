Facilitation of Olfactory Discrimination & Generalization Learning

This project and involves the development of simulated “field” training learning programs. These behavioral programs are expected to produce learning strategies that permit rodents to generalize the search and detection responses acquired within training chambers to live open environments including classrooms, offices, airports, train stations, etc.

(Funded by research support from Army Research Office to Williams, C. L., Univ. of Virginia and Barron Associates).
